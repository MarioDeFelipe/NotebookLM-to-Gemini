NotebookLM to Gemini

Welcome to the official homepage for the "NotebookLM to Gemini" Chrome Extension. This tool is designed to automate and enhance your research workflow by seamlessly connecting your knowledge base to the powerful analytical capabilities of Google Gemini.

Privacy Policy

This Chrome Extension, 'NotebookLM to Gemini', is designed to improve your productivity. It temporarily handles text you select on a webpage and sends it to Google's Gemini service when you initiate an action.

This extension does not store, save, or transmit any of your personal data or selected text to any servers other than Google's own Gemini service. All processing is done locally on your browser or between your browser and Google.

Created to bridge the gap between knowledge and analysis.

NotebookLM to Gemini Extension

Welcome to the official homepage for the NotebookLM to Gemini Chrome Extension. This tool automates your research workflow by connecting your knowledge base to the power of Google Gemini.

Privacy Policy

This extension does not store, save, or transmit any of your personal data or selected text to any servers other than Google's own Gemini service. All processing is done locally on your browser or between your browser and Google.
